The 7-Day Protocol

These meals support sustainable, healthy weight loss using simple ingredients and balanced nutrition. Prep once, execute daily.

The Power Scramble Breakfast

  • 2 whole eggs + 2 egg whites
  • 1 handful of fresh spinach
  • 1/4 diced bell pepper
  • 1 slice whole grain toast

Execution:

Spray a non-stick pan lightly with olive oil spray. Sauté the peppers until soft (3 mins). Throw in the spinach until wilted. Pour the whisked eggs and whites over the veg and scramble over medium heat. Serve with the dry toast.

Premium Upgrade

Add 2 slices of turkey bacon on the side for an extra 12g of protein with minimal calories.

Turkey Salad Bowl Lunch

  • 3 cups mixed greens or kale
  • 4 oz cooked turkey breast
  • Cherry tomatoes, cucumbers
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette

Execution:

Toss all ingredients into a massive mixing bowl. The goal here is volume. The dressing has calories, so stick exactly to 1 tbsp, but use unlimited vegetables.

Premium Upgrade

Swap turkey for 4oz of wild-caught salmon to add healthy Omega-3 fats for joint and brain health.

Lemon Herb Baked Chicken Dinner

  • 6 oz chicken breast
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 cup asparagus or broccoli
  • Lemon juice, oregano, salt, pepper

Execution:

Dice sweet potato. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 25 mins. Marinate chicken in lemon juice and oregano. Add the chicken and asparagus to the baking sheet for the final 15-18 mins until the chicken reaches 165°F internally.

Premium Upgrade

Use half a mashed avocado on the baked sweet potato instead of butter for satisfying, healthy fats.

Apple & Almonds Optional Snack

1 medium apple and exactly 14 raw almonds. Only consume this if you feel genuine, physical hunger between lunch and dinner. Do not eat it out of boredom.

Day 2: Sustained Energy

Overnight Protein Oats Breakfast

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Execution:

Mix everything in a jar or Tupperware. Seal it and put it in the fridge overnight. Grab and eat cold in the morning. Zero cooking time.

Premium Upgrade

Top with 1/4 cup of fresh raspberries in the morning for high-fiber antioxidants.

Tuna Salad Wraps Lunch

  • 1 can solid white tuna (in water)
  • 1 tbsp greek yogurt (instead of mayo)
  • 2 large lettuce or cabbage leaves
  • Diced celery and red onion

Execution:

Drain the tuna. Mix aggressively with greek yogurt, celery, and onion. Scoop the mixture into the lettuce leaves and eat like tacos.

Quick Shrimp Stir-Fry Dinner

  • 6 oz raw shrimp (peeled)
  • 2 cups frozen mixed stir-fry veggies
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil

Execution:

Cook the frozen veggies in a pan until warm. Push to the side. Add shrimp and cook 2 minutes per side until pink. Mix together, turn off heat, and toss in soy sauce and sesame oil immediately.

Premium Upgrade

Serve over 1/2 cup of cauliflower rice for massive volume without the carb crash.

Day 3: The Fat Burn Protocol

Berry Protein Smoothie Breakfast

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup berries
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Execution:

Blend everything together until smooth. The spinach adds micronutrients without altering the sweet berry flavor. Perfect for a fast morning.

Shrimp & Avocado Salad Lunch

  • 4 oz cooked shrimp
  • 3 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • Cherry tomatoes & cucumbers
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette

Execution:

Toss greens, tomatoes, cucumbers and avocado. Top with the cooked shrimp. Drizzle with balsamic just before eating to keep it crisp.

Turkey & Pepper Skillet Dinner

  • 5 oz ground or sliced turkey breast
  • 1 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce

Execution:

Sauté onions and peppers until soft. Add turkey and cook until browned. Toss with soy sauce for a savory finish.

Premium Upgrade

Serve over 1/2 roasted sweet potato for sustained evening energy.

Day 4: Digestive Reset

Avocado Egg Toast Breakfast

  • 2 whole eggs (fried or poached)
  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1/4 mashed avocado

Execution:

Toast the bread. Mash the avocado with a pinch of salt and spread it. Top with the eggs cooked to your preference.

Lemon Chicken Greens Lunch

  • 5 oz grilled chicken breast
  • 3 cups spinach or mixed greens
  • 10 raw almonds, crushed
  • Lemon juice & olive oil spray

Execution:

Slice the chicken over a massive bed of greens. Use a light spray of olive oil and heavy squeeze of fresh lemon as the dressing. Top with crushed almonds for crunch.

Tuna Celery Boats Dinner

  • 1 can tuna
  • 1 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 large celery stalks
  • Red onion, diced

Execution:

Mix tuna, yogurt, and onion. Wash the celery stalks and load the tuna mix directly into the natural curve of the celery for a massive crunch factor.

Day 5: High Protein Friday

Apple Cinnamon Oats Breakfast

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 diced apple
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein
  • 1 cup water / almond milk

Execution:

Cook the oats. Stir in the protein powder quickly to prevent clumping. Top immediately with diced crunchy apple segments.

Spicy Shrimp Cups Lunch

  • 5 oz cooked shrimp
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber & peppers
  • Soy sauce and sesame oil dash

Execution:

Toss shrimp and veggies in the soy/sesame mix. Scoop generously into lettuce leaves. Messy but high volume and extreme flavor.

Sweet Potato Chicken Hash Dinner

  • 5 oz diced chicken breast
  • 1 medium sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Olive oil spray

Execution:

Microwave sweet potato cubes for 3 minutes to soften. Then add to a hot pan with diced chicken. Sauté until crispy. Stir in spinach at the end.

Day 6: The Weekend Start

Tomato Spinach Omelet Breakfast

  • 3 egg whites, 1 whole egg
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup spinach

Execution:

Whisk eggs. Pour into a heated pan. Add halved tomatoes and spinach on one side, then fold the eggs over. Slide onto plate.

Turkey Protein Wrap Lunch

  • 5 oz turkey breast
  • 1 massive cabbage or lettuce leaf
  • Cucumber & tomato slices

Execution:

Lay out the massive leaf. Layer the turkey and veggies. Roll tightly and try to eat it without making a mess! Zero carb wrap.

Sesame Glazed Chicken Dinner

  • 6 oz chicken breast
  • 1 cup asparagus or broccoli
  • Soy sauce & Sesame oil mix

Execution:

Pan sear the chicken and veggies together. In the final minute of cooking, pour over the soy and sesame oil mix to create a sticky glaze.

Day 7: The Final Stretch

Vanilla Chia Bowl Breakfast

  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein
  • Berries for topping

Execution:

Mix chia, milk, and protein aggressively. Let sit for 10 minutes until it forms a thick pudding. Top with fresh berries.

Boiled Egg & Tuna Salad Lunch

  • 1 can tuna
  • 1 hard boiled egg, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • Bed of spinach

Execution:

Mix tuna, egg, and yogurt into a creamy high-protein salad. Serve it over a massive bed of spinach. Keeps you full for hours.

The Cleanout Stir-Fry Dinner

  • 3 oz chicken + 3 oz shrimp
  • Remaining Stir-fry mixed veg
  • Soy sauce

Execution:

Use whatever protein and veggies you have left in the fridge to ensure zero waste for the week. High heat, fast toss, simple soy sauce seasoning.

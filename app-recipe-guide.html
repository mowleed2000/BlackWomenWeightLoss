The Power Scramble

5 mins High Protein
Ingredients:
  • 2 whole eggs + 2 egg whites
  • Handful of spinach
  • 1/4 diced bell pepper

Instructions: Sauté peppers until soft. Add spinach. Whisk eggs and pour into pan. Scramble. Serve with avocado.

Overnight Protein Oats

2 mins (Prep) Zero Cooking
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

Instructions: Mix everything in a jar, seal, and leave in fridge overnight. Top with berries in the morning.

Turkey Salad Bowl

5 mins Volume Eating
Ingredients:
  • Mixed greens (spinach, kale)
  • 4 oz cooked turkey breast
  • Cherry tomatoes & cucumbers
  • 1 boiled egg

Instructions: Toss all ingredients into a massive bowl. Top with 1 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette.

Lemon Herb Baked Chicken

30 mins Comforting
Ingredients:
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • Lemon juice, oregano, salt

Instructions: Bake diced sweet potato at 400°F for 25 mins. Add seasoned chicken for the last 15 mins.

Quick Shrimp Stir-Fry

15 mins Metabolism
Ingredients:
  • 6 oz raw shrimp
  • 2 cups frozen mixed veggies
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil

Instructions: Cook veggies until soft. Add shrimp until pink (3-4 mins). Toss with soy sauce and sesame oil.

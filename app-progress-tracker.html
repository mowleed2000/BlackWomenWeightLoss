Before you log your first entry, understand why these specific metrics matter:

The Water Metric

Your liver requires massive amounts of water to metabolize fat. If you are dehydrated, fat burning physically halts. Aim for a minimum of 2.5 Liters (85 oz) daily. Thirst is often masked as hunger.

The Sleep Metric

Fat loss happens when you sleep, not when you train. If you sleep less than 6 hours, your body pumps out Cortisol (stress hormone), which causes you to retain water weight and crave sugar the next day. 7-8 hours is the non-negotiable standard.

Basic Food Logic

Fad diets fail because they ignore the law of thermodynamics. To lose fat, you must be in a Caloric Deficit (burning more energy than you consume). Achieve this by prioritizing Protein (keeps you full) and Vegetables (high volume, low calorie).

How to use this Logbook

  • Log your data every morning at the exact same time (after the bathroom, before breakfast).
  • Do not obsess over daily weight fluctuations. Track the 7-day average.
  • Be utterly honest. The data only judges your actions, not your worth.

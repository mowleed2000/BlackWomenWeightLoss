Lower Body Mechanics

The Bodyweight Squat No Equip

Correct Form: Stand shoulder-width apart. Hinge your hips back as if sitting in a chair. Keep chest tall, back straight. Drive through your heels to return to standing while squeezing glutes. Do not let knees cave inwards.
🔰 Beginner Edit

Box Squats: Squat down until your glutes touch a chair or couch, then stand back up. Reduces balance requirement.

⚡ Advanced Edit

Jump Squats: Explode upward from the bottom of the squat until feet leave the floor. Absorb impact silently.

Reverse Lunges No Equip

Correct Form: Stand tall. Step one foot backward and lower your hips until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle. The back knee should hover just above the floor. Push off the back foot to return to the start.
🔰 Beginner Edit

Hold onto a wall, chair, or doorway for balance while dropping the knee.

⚡ Advanced Edit

Bulgarian Split Squats: Elevate the rear foot on a chair or couch. Massively increases glute isolation.

Upper Body Strength

The Strict Pushup No Equip

Correct Form: Hands directly under shoulders. Keep your body in a perfectly straight line from head to heels. Lower your chest until it is an inch from the floor, elbows tucked back at a 45-degree angle (not flaring out). Push the floor violently away.
🔰 Beginner Edit

Incline Pushups: Place hands on a sturdy chair, counter, or wall. Do not drop to elbows; maintain the straight body line.

⚡ Advanced Edit

Decline Pushups: Elevate your feet on a chair. Shifts weight forward, increasing chest and delt load.

Tricep Dips Chair / Couch

Correct Form: Sit on the edge of a sturdy chair. Place hands next to hips, fingers pointing forward. Slide hips off the chair. Bend elbows to lower your body until upper arms are parallel to the floor. Push back up forcefully.
🔰 Beginner Edit

Keep feet flat on the floor, knees bent at 90 degrees to assist with the push.

⚡ Advanced Edit

Straighten your legs completely, resting only your heels on the floor.

Core Validation

The Forearm Plank No Equip

Correct Form: Elbows directly beneath shoulders. Body perfectly straight from head to heels. Squeeze your glutes violently and pull your belly button up towards your spine. Do not let your lower back sag.
🔰 Beginner Edit

Knee Plank: Drop your knees to the floor but maintain the straight line from head to knees.

⚡ Advanced Edit

Plank Up-Downs: Push up from forearms to hands, then lower back to forearms, minimizing hip sway.

Bicycle Crunches No Equip

Correct Form: Lie flat. Press lower back firmly into the floor. Bring knees to tabletop. Place hands behind head lightly. Extend right leg while bringing right elbow to left knee. Switch sides in a controlled, pedaling motion.
🔰 Beginner Edit

Heel Taps: Keep knees bent, tap one heel to the floor at a time while keeping the lower back pressed down.

⚡ Advanced Edit

Hold for 2 seconds on every 'twist' to force maximum oblique contraction.

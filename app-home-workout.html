Interactive Home Workouts

No gym. No equipment. Just effort.

Lower Body Focus 3 Sets

Air Squats, Reverse Lunges, Glute Bridges

1. Air Squats: 12-15 reps. Push through your heels.

2. Reverse Lunges: 10 reps per leg.

3. Glute Bridges: 15 reps. Squeeze at the top.

Upper Body & Core 3 Sets

Pushups, Planks, Bicycle Crunches

1. Incline / Knee Pushups: 8-12 reps.

2. Plank Holds: 30-45 seconds.

3. Bicycle Crunches: 15-20 reps total.

The Cardio Finisher Burn

Jumping Jacks, High Knees, Burpees (Optional)

Follow along: Do each movement for 40 seconds, rest for 20 seconds. Repeat 3 times.
